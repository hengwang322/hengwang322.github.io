Projects

Wind Power Prediction

I developed a wind power prediction model to forecast hourly outputs of major wind farms in South Australia. First I built a data pipeline in Python to acquire publicly available data. After cleaning the data and some EDA, an XGBoost model is built to discover the relationship between the weather and power data. Finally, I developed a live demo hosted on Heroku to continuously make forecasts based on real-time weather data.
